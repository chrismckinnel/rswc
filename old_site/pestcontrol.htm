Pest Control

Human sling load for deep bush trend monitoring

Industry leaders delivering excellence in strategic and integrated pest control solutions.

VCS was originally established as a business unit of the West Coast Regional Council in xxxx to undertake Tb vector control (possum control) contracts within the Animnal Health Board's Bovine Tb control programs on the West Coast. While the business scope has expanded, pest control still makes up a significant aspect of our operations.

 

We are currently carrying out:

  • Aerial based possum control operations
  • Ground based possum control operations
  • Trap Catch monitoring services
  • Deep bush trend monitoring
    (Click to view video)
  • Chew card monitoring services
  • Wax Tag monitoring services
  • Pest plant spraying
  • Providing support services to Landcare Research
  • Didymo surveying and public awareness
